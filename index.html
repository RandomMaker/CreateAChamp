March 21st at Wilfrid Laurier University

An opportunity for Gold

Badminton Canada is launching a nation-wide program called Pathways. The aim of this program is to engage and enrich those who play the sport across Canada. Other programs such as Pathways have proven that the structure it brings is key to the development of Olympic champions.

Project Gold is a competition that brings together the best and brightest students and professionals. Put your skills to the test in a fun and interactive environment that is for a national cause.

Competition Overview

Project Gold is not your traditional case competition. We've designed it to be engaging and use more than just your business analyzing skills.

During the event day you will work with your groups to develop a proposal that target specific aspects of Badminton Canada's marketing initiatives. If you don't have a group coming in, do not worry, we will form one for you.

The case will be released prior to the event date and you will be allowed to work on it ahead of time. We encourage you to look over it at minimum so that you have a good grasp.
